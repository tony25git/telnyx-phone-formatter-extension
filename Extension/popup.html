Phone Number Formatter

Custom Formats

Use X for digits. 10 X's: Individual formatting (no ranges). Example: XXX*XXX*XXXX. 14+ X's: Smart range formatting. Example: XXX*XXX*XXXX*XXXX (ranges as 987*654*3210*3211). 20 X's: Full range format (XX/XXX/XXXXX/XX/XXX/XXXXX).

Manage Custom Formats

Instructions:

  • Enter phone numbers in any format (with at least 10 digits)
  • Uses the rightmost 10 digits of each number
  • 888-888-8888: Dash format (with ranges)
  • 888 888 8888: Space format (with ranges)
  • 8888888888-8888: Range format
  • +1: Add +1 prefix (no ranges)
  • 1: Add 1 prefix (no ranges)
  • Simple: Plain 10-digit numbers (no ranges)
  • Copy: Copy all numbers to clipboard
